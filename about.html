About Us

Curious Kiwi was established in Hong Kong by our nomadic CEO. His aim was to build a company around providing amazing solutions for clients and building out complex, scalable web applications. If we had stopped there though, we'd be just like many of our competitors out there...so we didn't.

Curious Kiwi's core goal is simple: To grow into an international company that both provides great value for our clients, while never losing sight of supporting and growing our team, and doing it all on our own terms from every corner of the globe.

We've had team members working from six of the seven continients. Still trying to figure out how to work from Antarctica, but we'll get there.
