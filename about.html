About Us

Curious Kiwi's core goal is simple: We provide web development and server maintenence for small to medium size business owners because we know that having those things in place in today's global, continuously-connected, internet-age is what can make or break a comapny.


Curious Kiwi was established in by our nomadic CEO with the aim to build a company around providing amazing web support solutions for clients; helping to develop, maintain, upgrade, and troubleshoot web applications of all shapes and sizes. If we had stopped there though, we'd be just like many of our competitors out there...so we didn't.


We are doing it all on our own terms from almost every corner of the globe. We've had team members working from six of the seven continients. Still trying to figure out how to work from Antarctica, but we'll get there. To say that we're excited about what the future has in store is an understatement!


Our vision is to help support 1,000 more businesses in growing and maintaining their websites by 2020! Will yours be one of them?


We know you have great ideas for your website. Let us help you get there! We pride ourselves on delivering the best quality services at great prices to help keep your website running to let you focus on what you do best, getting your business out to the world.
